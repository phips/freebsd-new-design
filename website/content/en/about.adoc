"FreeBSD" can mean several things depending on the context. Here are a few links to get you to the sources that describe each.

* link:https://docs.freebsd.org/en/books/handbook/introduction/#nutshell[The Operating System] - most people mean the _operating system_ when they say or write "FreeBSD." The FreeBSD Handbook contains general information about the OS as well as a complete guide for using it.
* link:https://docs.freebsd.org/en/books/handbook/introduction/#history[The Project] - the _FreeBSD Project_ is the collection of efforts to develop and release the FreeBSD OS, maintain ports and packages, and provide documentation for it all.
* link:/administration/[Project Administration] - the _project administration_ team ensures the FreeBSD Project makes progress in an orderly manner
* link:https://freebsdfoundation.org/about-us/about-the-foundation/[The Foundation] - the _FreeBSD Foundation_ is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting and promoting the FreeBSD Project and community worldwide
